1. Product Essence

A web-based financial coordination tool for recurring Pickleball sessions that transparently manages shared costs, clarifies real-time balances per member, and tracks financial positions over time to support fair, low-friction group dynamics.

2. Core Problem

The group lacks:

A persistent individual financial view

Temporal insight into payment behavior

Structured accountability over time

This produces delayed settlements, memory bias, and interpersonal discomfort.

3. Updated Product Objective

To deliver a system that:

Computes fair session-based cost splits

Displays real-time personal financial standing

Tracks owed and owing trends over time

Preserves social harmony via neutral visualization

4. Core Functional Architecture
4.1 Session Layer

Create Pickleball session

Log expenses (court, water, balls, etc.)

Assign payer

Auto-split equally among participants

Generate session ledger

4.2 Member Dashboard Layer (Key Addition)

Each user has a personalized dashboard displaying:

Financial Status Summary

Total amount to pay

Total amount to receive

Net balance position (positive / negative)

Debt Breakdown

List of who the member owes

List of who owes the member

Amount per relationship

Time View

Balance by week / month

Payment history timeline

Outstanding duration per debt

5. Time Tracking System
Time Dimensions Captured

Session date

Payment date

Debt creation timestamp

Debt age (days outstanding)

Time-Based Insights

Oldest unpaid balance

Repayment frequency

Average settlement time

Financial trend over time

6. Vibe Intent (Emotional Objective)

Primary emotional targets:

Fairness

Control

Transparency

Calm accountability

Psychological safety

7. Vibe Attributes
Emotional Goal	Design Translation
Control	Clear hierarchy, structured layout
Transparency	Open data visibility, no hidden metrics
Calm accountability	Non-judgmental tone, soft indicators
Trust	Predictable flows, consistent visuals
8. Dashboard Vibe Direction
Visual Aesthetic

Structured with breathing space

Calm, analytical tone

Muted neutral backdrop with signal highlights

Emphasis on clarity over emotional triggers

Interaction Vibe

Status indicators instead of warnings
Example microcopy:

“Balance currently unsettled for 4 sessions”

“You are in a receiving position of 250,000 VND”

Time indicators phrased factually, not critically:

“Outstanding since 12 Apr 2025”

“Average settlement time: 3.2 days”

9. Dashboard Information Hierarchy

Net Position (Hero Metric)

Amount To Pay / To Receive

Debt Relationships

Time Status & Trends

Session History

10. Vibe Rules for Time Representation

Avoid red/green moral signals

Use neutral progress indicators

Represent time as informative, not accusatory

Prioritize calm progression visuals over countdown pressure

11. Experience Principles

No shame mechanics

System-first communication

Data clarity before emotion

Gentle nudge, never pressure

12. Success Indicators

Members check dashboards without hesitation

Balances are settled faster

Reduced interpersonal reminders

Increased trust in system neutrality
